Property Description

Wasl village is an embodiment of ideal living. It is a contemporary lifestyle residential development nestled in Al Qusais, located right off Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road and Beirut Street.

Constituting multiple residential and mixed-use buildings comprising studio, 1-,2- and 3-bedroom apartments, wasl village is a renting project that perfectly blends peace and a distinct sense of communal belonging. It encourages an active lifestyle by offering a walking trail within the community; a 1,700-metre jogging track circling the project; premier fitness equipment; cricket, football and basketball court; and open spaces of green landscapes.

The project has ample car parking spaces and is in proximity to a school, a retail centre, a mosque, play areas, and more. Ideally located within reach of Dubai and Sharjah’s major roadways and catering to families and working professionals, wasl village is a self-sufficient community that is also surrounded by several residential developments.
