ABOUT
PROJECT

Hyatt Palace is a multi-faceted project consisting of multiple residential and mixed-use buildings, featuring studio, 1-, 2-, and 3-bedroom apartments. This exceptional rental project seamlessly combines serenity with a strong sense of community. It promotes an active lifestyle through various amenities, including a picturesque walking trail within the community, a 1,700-meter jogging track encircling the project, state-of-the-art fitness equipment, as well as cricket, football, and basketball courts. Additionally, the project offers vast expanses of lush green landscapes, providing residents with an ideal environment for relaxation and recreation.
