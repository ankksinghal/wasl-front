Property description

Nad Al Hammar Gardens is the latest venture from Wasl, one of the largest real estate management and development companies in Dubai established by the Dubai Real Estate Corporation (DREC) which is backed by more than three decades of immaculate reputation, cutting edge construction and exemplary after sales service.

Wasl manages an extensive real estate portfolio of over 30,000 residential and commercial properties within the emirate of Dubai. The company’s operations also spans various sectors including industrial plots, leisure and entertainment, retail and hospitality projects.

Its first foray into buying was the Hyatt Regency Creek Heights Residences, a 43-storied contemporary tower located by Dubai Creek and offering luxury apartments, super luxury loft duplexes and dedicated hotel services, managed by the Hyatt group.

Wasl will provide a top-class infrastructure in the Nad Al Hammar Gardens project, resulting in a well-planned and connected community for future residents and owners.

Gardenia Townhomes II

Due to the success of Wasl Gate, Wasl launches Gardenia Townhomes II, a new phase which offers a selection of spacious 3 and 4-bedroom townhomes, perfectly combining luxury and comfortable living, nestled amidst a lush and welcoming neighborhood.

With contemporary architecture and premium amenities, Gardenia Townhomes II provides a vibrant lifestyle within a relaxing, gated community. Each home is designed to feature spacious bedrooms and living areas, as well as private gardens.

Additionally, residents can enjoy a brand-new community swimming pool, and full access to the clubhouse with fully-equipped gym and kids play area. Gardenia Townhomes II will be a pet-friendly community and will include an adjacent pet park. The development is also within walking distance from Festival Plaza mall that includes IKEA & ACE.
