History

Wasl was established on May 25, 2008 by the Dubai Real Estate Corporation (DREC) to oversee the management of its assets and grow its real estate portfolio. Its main objective is to strengthen Dubai’s position as the premier hub to live and work in and at the same time be the ultimate destination for tourists.

Created following the merger of two government organisations; Dubai Development Board and Real Estate Department, Wasl has focused its activities on a number of key business sectors including project and property management, hospitality and investment management.

The successful merger and integration included changing business practices, modifying core competencies of people and implementing market driven investment strategies. Today Wasl is one of the largest real estate management companies in Dubai.

Wasl operates across a wide spectrum of sectors through its large portfolio of real estate assets that it manages on behalf of DREC and others. These sectors range from residential and commercial properties, to industrial plots of land and, from leisure and entertainment to hotels and serviced apartments.

Investment Philosophy

As an asset management group, Wasl’s primary objective is to manage its portfolio with care, discipline and patience. Wasl’s investment strategy is focused on delivering compelling returns while protecting and growing the asset value over the long term.

Wasl's real estate portfolio is significant in scale and broadly diversified, encompassing residential, office, hotel, retail, industrial, healthcare and multi-purpose properties across Dubai. The Company has the largest portfolio of residential units in Dubai and includes many of the emirate’s best known landmarks, such the Grand Hyatt Dubai, Le Méridien Mina Seyahi Beach Resort & Marina, the Westin Dubai Mina Seyahi Beach Resort & Marina, Dubai Creek Golf & Yacht Club, Emirates Golf Club, samari residences and our latest development Wasl square.

The long-term view and the balanced diversification of the portfolio allow us to adjust Wasl’s commercial risk, ride out market cycles and maximise value through operational improvements over time.

Our investment approach is value-oriented, focusing on real estate sectors that will support the growth of Dubai and further enhance its position as the destination of choice. Wasl targets opportunities for equity investment, mergers and acquisitions as well as active engagement in operating businesses that offer the potential for vertical or horizontal integration and are consistent with our overall strategy of developing transferable knowledge and expertise.

Executive Team

His Highness Sheikh Maktoum bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum is the Chairman of Wasl. He leads the Board and provides long-term strategic direction ensuring that Wasl continues to be in line with Dubai’s economic plan.

He is supported by His Excellency Hesham Al Qassim, Wasl’s Chief Executive Officer, who also acts as the Vice Chairman of DREC. H.E. Al Qassim is responsible for the strategic development of the group and the management of its real estate, hospitality and leisure portfolio. To fulfil Wasl’s mission, he continuously looks for and targets niche market gaps and focuses on opportunities that complement Dubai’s urban growth.

H.E. Al Qassim leads a team of highly experienced professionals from the real estate industry and other related sectors. Wasl's management team has the expertise required to direct the company’s day-to-day operations and growth strategy while continually exploring innovative ways to meet the needs of customers and maximise the value to its shareholder.
