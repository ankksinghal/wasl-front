How can I lease an apartment?

To lease an apartment, please follow below steps:

1. For leasing through the App:

  • 1. Download our leasing app from Apple or Google store
  • 2. Click on account and select sign up
  • 3. Sign up with your UAE Pass or fill your credentials
  • 4. Agree on the terms and conditions and click create an account
  • 5. Click on search and select filter from the top to choose your preferences
  • 6. Click on the building that you wish to explore
  • 7. Click select, it will list down available apartments
  • 8. Select the unit you want to view and click view
  • 9. Click on the map to get direction for viewing (Note: viewing hours are from 9 AM To 6 PM daily)
  • 10. Post viewing select the apartment you want to lease and click "Lease Now." (Note: Current tenants are allowed to lease other units subject to approval, when selecting lease a call back request will be created and you will be contacted by the leasing team within 48 hours to help you with the exception), (Note: Booking is not available, we lease by first come first serve only)
  • 11. Fill in the following details: Job Title, Marital Status, Monthly Income (Note: Monthly instalment must not cross 50% of your salary, if it's more, the system will create a call back request for you and the leasing team will call you within 48 Hours to advise on the next steps)
  • 12. Agree to the terms and conditions and click continue (Note: If you are single, a call back ticket will be created for you and the leasing team will contact you within 48 hours to help you with your lease as most of our buildings are for families only)
  • 13. The leasing team will request you to sign an undertaken document that will be shared with you
  • 14. You will be requested to share the ID, Passport & Visas of the first-degree relatives that are going to be living with you
  • 15. Confirm the new lease and input the OTP (One-Time Password) received via SMS.
  • 16. Click "Continue."
  • 17. Upload all the required documents below and Click "Continue."
  • 18. Review the Payment Summary and choose the number of instalment from 1, 2, Or 12 & Click "Continue."
  • 19. Choose the payment mode (Card or cheque) & Click "Continue. (Note: First payment must be from a debit or a credit card, chequs are not allowed for first payment)
  • 20. Post choosing the payment, you will be redirected to the Payment Gateway page.
  • 21. Input the details of your Card.
  • 22. Click "Next" to complete the transaction.
